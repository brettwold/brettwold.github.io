Live Wallpapers

I have developed several live wallpaper applications for many of the major film studios. These personalisation applications allow handset owners to customise the backdrop of any Android handset with the style and characters from their favourite films. Often these wallpapers consist of a challenging mix 2D drawing and high definition assets. Each one is customised to create a unique effect that is appropriate to the film in question. The software developed must take care of the various screen sizes and memory limits of Android devices in the current market. These wallpapers present an extra challenge due to the limited resources available from the handsets as they are designed to run in the background whilst not effect the core functionality of the handset themselves.
